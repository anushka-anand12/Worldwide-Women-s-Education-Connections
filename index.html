Worldwide Women's Education Connections

Welcome!

When was the last time you complained about having homework to do, or having to go to school? The majority of students would say that they can remember a time when they would rather be anywhere but school; however, education is a privilege that we often overlook. Millions of women across the globe do not have access to quality education. We wanted to solve this problem by creating the Worldwide Women's Education Connections program, which is a program that allows women to find resource centers in their area, connect with online teachers, and find online resources to further their education needs. We hope you will support us in our fight to educate the women of today!


How to Navigate our Site

Are you looking for affordable ways to begin or expand your education? Head on over to our Educational Resources tab!


Are you looking for a place to help sponsor women and nonbinary people in need of an education? Head on over to our Donate page!


Do you know someone who is in need of a sponsor, or do you yourself need an education sponsor? Head over to our Contact Us Page, where you can reach out to one of our team members for assistance. We are more than happy to help you kickstart your learning experience!
