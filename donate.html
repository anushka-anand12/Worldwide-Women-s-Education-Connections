Commit2Change: C2C is an organization that aims to educate girls in orphanages in India. It works with carefully selected local partners who rescue and rehabilitate at-risk girls and invest in initiatives to give them a chance. C2C has a variety of programs such as the cutting edge C2C Tech Lab, that teaches girls the basics of coding and entrepreunerships. Visit their website today if you would like a chance to turn your life around.

Girl Up: Girl Up is a program founded by the United Nations that empowers women to fight for social justice and equal education oppurtunities.

School Girls Unite : School Girls Unite is a donation-run program that offers education scholarships to girls globally. Their program helps pay for a student’s tuition, supplies, and tutors. Start your own School Girls Unite chapter in your community or school by visiting their website. Additionally, help fill out this petition to make a difference!

The Malala Fund: Malala Yousafzai, the 24 year-old Pakistani activist who spent her life advocating for girls education has a fund that “invests in education activists and advocates who are driving solutions to barriers to girls’ education in their communities”. Malala Fund is a great source to donate to because they speak with world leaders, include girls in the conversation, and support grassroot activists. For instance, they call on developing countries to spend 20% of their budget on education and donor countries to commit 15% of their aid to education.
